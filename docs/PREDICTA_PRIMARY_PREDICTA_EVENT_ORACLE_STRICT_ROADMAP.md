# Predicta Primary Intelligence And Precision Event Oracle Strict Roadmap

This roadmap makes Predicta the main character of the app and upgrades the app
from a set of astrology rooms with a chat sidekick into a prediction-first
astrology intelligence system.

The specialist worlds still remain. Vedic, KP, Jaimini, Numerology, Signature,
Kundli Karma, Reports, and Life Atlas become evidence rooms and depth surfaces.
The primary user experience becomes:

> Ask Predicta first. Predicta understands the question, chooses the right
> evidence rooms, gives the direct prediction, then exposes proof only when the
> user wants it.

This roadmap is not a replacement for report, monetization, Jaimini, Kundli
Karma, AI model, or UI audit roadmaps. It is a stricter product-intelligence
spine that coordinates them around one central promise: Predicta gives clear,
specific, evidence-backed guidance without fear, fluff, or per-minute pressure.

1. AstroLokal's public edge is real astrologer access, quick paid sessions,

2. Predicta's strongest future is one primary Predicta intelligence layer using

## Locked Product Position

Predicta is the primary intelligence layer and focal point of the app.

Predicta is:

> Your personal astrology intelligence. She reads across your Kundli, timing,
> karma patterns, numbers, specialist systems, and available evidence to give
> clear guidance.

Predicta Precision Reading is:

> Evidence-backed astrology prediction for one important life question, checked
> across Vedic, KP, Jaimini, dasha, transit, timing indicators, and available
> supporting systems.

Specialist worlds are:

- `Vedic`: life foundation, dasha, charts, yogas, karma, remedies.
- `KP`: precise event questions, promise/block, cusp/sub-lord evidence, timing.
- `Jaimini`: soul path, destiny, karakas, Arudha, Chara Dasha, life direction.
- `Numerology`: name/date rhythm, personal cycles, timing support.
- `Signature`: expression and self-presentation, only when signature exists.
- `Kundli Karma`: Dosh, Shrap, Yog, Lal Kitab, karmic friction/support.
- `Life Atlas`: full synthesis report, not the default chat replacement.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

1. Predicta is the primary intelligence and focal point of the app.
2. Specialist worlds remain available, but they become evidence/depth rooms
   instead of competing main experiences.
3. Do not silently mix schools. Predicta must disclose which evidence rooms she
   used when synthesis crosses methods.
4. If the user is inside a specialist world, Predicta must stay room-safe unless
   the user explicitly asks for synthesis.
5. The main Predicta chat can synthesize across Vedic, KP, Jaimini, Numerology,
   Signature, Kundli Karma, Reports, and Life Atlas.
6. Prediction comes first. Education, toolkit language, and technical proof come
   after the direct answer.
7. Every event answer must include a direct answer, timing window, likely
   trigger, confidence, delay factors, strengthening factors, next action, and
   optional evidence.
8. Do not claim `100% guarantee`, `definitely`, or fate certainty.
9. Do not weaken predictions into vague generic advice. Specificity is required
   where evidence supports it.
10. If evidence conflicts, Predicta must say so and lower confidence instead of
    forcing a false answer.
11. Health, legal, finance, and safety topics require careful disclaimers and
    practical non-deterministic guidance.
12. Human astrologers may review Predicta outputs, but humans must not freestyle
    fear-based or unsupported predictions.
13. Human review must see deterministic evidence, contradiction notes, timing
    windows, and the drafted Predicta answer before approval/refinement.
14. No forced remedies, fear-selling, expensive puja pressure, or manipulative
    recharge loops.
15. Every paid session must produce a saved transcript/summary unless the user
    explicitly deletes it.
16. Every prediction must be trackable later so the user can mark whether it
    happened, partially happened, did not happen, or is still pending.
17. AI cost must be controlled by deterministic routing, credit gates, concise
    free answers, and paid precision products.
18. If local deterministic memory can answer, Predicta must avoid AI usage.
19. No phase is green from code review alone.
20. UI phases require desktop, tablet, mobile, and narrow-mobile evidence.
21. Report-affecting phases require generated free and paid artifacts where
    applicable.
22. Each phase must be strictly audited, fixed, verified, and committed before
    the next phase starts.
23. No roadmap may silently conflict with this one. If a conflict is found, the
    conflicting roadmap or gate must be updated explicitly.

## Phase 0: `PREDICTA_EVENT_ORACLE_PHASE_0_ASTROLOKAL_BENCHMARK_AND_PRIMARY_PREDICTA_SCOPE_LOCK`

### Goal

Lock the new product standard before implementation: Predicta must become the
primary intelligence layer and must answer real event questions more precisely
than generic report/chat competitors.

### Must Audit

- Current dashboard/home emphasis: is `Ask Predicta` visually primary?
- Current specialist-world routing: do worlds compete with Predicta or support
  her?
- Current chat answers: do they give direct answers, timing, and triggers?
- Current reports/previews: do they school the user or predict for the user?
- AstroLokal public positioning: real astrologers, paid sessions, language,
  event topics, privacy, pricing signals, review praise/complaints.
- Existing Predicta monetization: question credits, reports, passes, and where
  Precision Reading can fit without breaking cost controls.

### Must Produce

- Primary Predicta scope ledger.
- AstroLokal benchmark matrix.
- Event-prediction gap ledger.
- Roadmap conflict ledger.
- No-go criteria for vague predictions, silent school mixing, and sidekick chat.

### Green Criteria

- Product scope is locked in writing.
- Existing roadmap conflicts are identified.
- No implementation begins until the scope and redlines are documented.
- Commit includes the baseline evidence.

## Phase 3: `PREDICTA_EVENT_ORACLE_PHASE_3_MULTI_SCHOOL_EVIDENCE_CONTRACT_AND_CONFLICT_SCHEMA`

### Goal

Create the shared evidence contract that lets Predicta compare multiple schools
without hallucinating or silently mixing.

### Must Implement

Each event prediction must support these evidence layers where available:

- Vedic: dasha, antardasha, pratyantardasha, transit, house lords, D1, Moon,
  D9, D10, Chalit, yogas, Dosh/Shrap/Yog/Lal Kitab.
- KP: relevant houses, cusps, star lord, sub lord, significators, ruling
  planets, promise/block, timing readiness.
- Jaimini: karakas, Arudha, Karakamsha, Upapada, Chara Dasha, rashi drishti.
- Numerology: personal year/month/day, name rhythm, timing color.
- Signature: confirmed traits only, never required, never invented.
- Life Atlas: synthesis context only after source layers exist.

### Must Include

- Agreement score.
- Conflict score.
- Missing-evidence flags.
- Evidence source labels.
- Method boundaries.
- Confidence explanation.
- `not enough evidence` state.

### Green Criteria

- Evidence contract is deterministic and typed.
- School-specific proof remains source-aware.
- Conflicting evidence lowers confidence.
- Missing evidence is never filled by AI invention.

## Phase 4: `PREDICTA_EVENT_ORACLE_PHASE_4_PRECISION_TIMING_TRIGGER_AND_CONFIDENCE_ENGINE`

### Goal

Turn evidence into user-valuable prediction objects.

### Must Implement

Every event answer must include:

- `directAnswer`
- `timingWindow`
- `mostLikelyTrigger`
- `confidence`
- `whatCanDelayIt`
- `whatCanStrengthenIt`
- `whatToDoNow`
- `collapsedEvidence`

Timing windows must be honest:

- exact date only when evidence supports it
- otherwise month range, quarter, dasha window, or `not precise yet`
- no fake certainty

### Green Criteria

- Prediction objects are specific enough to be useful.
- Confidence is evidence-backed.
- Trigger language is practical and real-world oriented.
- No answer claims guaranteed outcomes.

## Phase 7: `PREDICTA_EVENT_ORACLE_PHASE_7_PREDICTION_FIRST_LANGUAGE_AND_NO_SCHOOLING_GATE`

### Goal

Remove toolkit/schooling tone from event answers and make prediction language
clear, direct, and helpful.

### Must Enforce

Answers start with what the user wants to know:

- likely / delayed / blocked / mixed / needs clarity
- timing window
- trigger
- action

Technical language is allowed only after the answer.

### Must Reject

- "This house represents..."
- "KP uses..."
- "This report helps you understand..."
- generic life-coaching filler
- implementation-contract language
- over-educational toolkit copy

### Green Criteria

- Red-team prompts across all event categories pass.
- Free answers are useful but concise.
- Paid answers are deeper, not more verbose filler.
- Predictions are not fear-based.

- Free and paid entitlement proof.
- Zero-credit deterministic mode proof.
