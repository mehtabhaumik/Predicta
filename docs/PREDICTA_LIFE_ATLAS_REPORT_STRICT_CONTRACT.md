# Predicta Life Atlas Report Strict Contract

This document defines the standalone contract for the `Predicta Life Atlas`
report.

`Predicta Life Atlas` is a synthesis report. It is not a Vedic report, not a KP
report, not a Jaimini report, not a Numerology report, and not a Signature
report.
It is the only approved report family where Predicta may combine multiple
schools into one human life story.

## Product Name

Approved report name:

- `Predicta Life Atlas`

Alternative names considered but not approved as the primary product name:

- `Soul Blueprint Report`
- `Life Journey & Soul Purpose Report`
- `Predicta Destiny Dossier`

## Core Positioning

`Predicta Life Atlas` is a mystical, emotional, practical, non-technical life
report. It should feel like a premium personal soul-purpose dossier that helps a
user understand their life journey, destiny pattern, current chapter, gifts,
lessons, and next direction.

The report must translate technical evidence into human language.

Do not write:

- `Saturn in house 6 causes this.`
- `Your Rahu placement guarantees this outcome.`
- `The KP cusp proves this will happen.`

Write instead:

- `Your life repeatedly asks you to become steady under pressure.`
- `You are learning to turn responsibility into mastery.`
- `Delay in your path is not always denial; often it is refinement.`

## Non-Negotiable Rules

1. `Predicta Life Atlas` must remain separate from school-specific reports.
2. This report is the only approved synthesis report path in this contract.
3. Vedic, KP, Jaimini, Numerology, and Signature reports must not silently include
   `Predicta Life Atlas` sections.
4. `Predicta Life Atlas` may use Vedic, KP, Jaimini, and Numerology as core
   evidence layers.
5. Signature analysis is optional enrichment only.
6. The report must still generate when no signature sample exists.
7. Signature-based language must only appear when a real signature sample or
   confirmed manual observation exists.
8. Do not invent signature traits such as slant, pressure, baseline, spacing,
   legibility, rhythm, underline, flourish, size, or consistency.
9. The report must not claim access to Akashic Records, palm-leaf manuscripts,
   spirit guides, divine archives, or any source Predicta does not actually
   access.
10. The report must not guarantee events, fixed destiny, death, illness, wealth,
    marriage, divorce, childbirth, or any irreversible life outcome.
11. The report must not use fear-heavy, fatalistic, or manipulative language.
12. The report must not expose technical proof in the main reading.
13. Premium may include a technical-light appendix called
    `How Predicta Built This Reading`, but it must remain user-friendly.
14. Free reports must be genuinely useful, not a hollow teaser.
15. Premium/paid reports must add depth, synthesis, specificity, integration,
    and practical guidance without making impossible certainty claims.
16. Predicta memory must know this report exists, what it contains, what data
    powered it, and what was omitted because data was unavailable.

## Available Data Hierarchy

Expected core inputs:

- Vedic data from birth details and calculated Kundli.
- KP data from the same birth profile where KP preparation is available.
- Jaimini evidence for soul role, visible identity, career dharma,
  relationship mirror, and current destiny chapter.
- Numerology data from name and date of birth.

Optional enrichment:

- Signature analysis from a real uploaded/drawn signature sample or confirmed
  manual observation.

If signature is missing, the report must show a tasteful note:

```text
Signature expression layer was not included because no signature sample was provided.
```

Premium may invite the user to add signature later:

```text
Add your signature to enrich the expression and self-presentation layer.
```

must stay non-technical.

```text
Predicta synthesized your birth profile, timing rhythm, number pattern, and available expression signals into one life story.
```

If signature is unavailable, use:

```text
Predicta synthesized your birth profile, timing rhythm, and number pattern into one life story.
```

## Free Report Depth

Free `Predicta Life Atlas` must be useful and emotionally resonant.

Expected free structure:

- Approximately 8 to 12 polished pages.
- Clear soul portrait.
- One-page life journey summary.
- Current life chapter.
- Top 3 gifts.
- Top 3 lessons.
- One `What to focus on now` section.
- Short closing letter from Predicta.

Free must not:

- feel cheap
- withhold all meaningful insight
- dump technical proof
- pressure the user with fear
- promise that Premium reveals guaranteed fate

## Premium/Paid Report Depth

Premium/paid `Predicta Life Atlas` is the flagship life report.

There is no fixed page cap. Report length is determined by meaningful coverage,
readability, pagination safety, and available data.

Premium/paid must include:

- Deep life journey narrative.
- Full soul-purpose synthesis.
- Current chapter and next phase.
- Love, work, money, and purpose synthesis.
- Karmic pattern map.
- Shadow-to-gift transformation.
- Detailed practices and integration plan.
- Optional signature expression layer only when signature data exists.
- Premium closing letter that feels personal, sacred, and unforgettable.
- Optional appendix: `How Predicta Built This Reading`.

Premium/paid must not:

- become a technical astrology report
- overclaim exact events
- pretend missing signature data exists
- make the user feel doomed
- replace practical judgment, professional advice, or personal agency

## Language Rules

Use mystical but grounded language:

- `soul blueprint`
- `life pattern`
- `destiny direction`
- `what your chart and symbolic systems suggest`
- `the path your life keeps inviting you toward`
- `current chapter`
- `hidden thread`
- `gifts you carry`
- `lessons asking to mature`

Avoid unsupported or overclaiming language:

- `we accessed your Akashic Records`
- `this is guaranteed`
- `this will definitely happen`
- `your soul came only for this one reason`
- `you cannot escape this fate`
- `this proves your future`
- `your signature shows this` when no signature exists
