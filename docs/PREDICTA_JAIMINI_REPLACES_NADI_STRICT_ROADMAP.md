# Predicta Jaimini Replaces Nadi Strict Roadmap

This roadmap replaces `Nadi Predicta` with `Jaimini Predicta` across Predicta.
It is not a rename-only phase. It is a full school migration, data-contract
rebuild, UI/report rebuild, Life Atlas integration, intelligence-memory update,
and strict audit program.

The goal is to make Jaimini feel classical, precise, soulful, calm, and
valuable from day one. Predicta must not repeat the earlier mistake of shipping
a specialist room that looks polished but reads like an internal method note,
toolkit, astrology lesson, or generic filler report.

## Product Decision

`Nadi Predicta` is removed from the five specialist-room model.

The new five specialist rooms are:

1. `Vedic Predicta`
2. `KP Predicta`
3. `Jaimini Predicta`
4. `Numerology Predicta`
5. `Signature Predicta`

`Predicta Life Atlas` remains the separate synthesis report. Life Atlas may use
Jaimini evidence only after Jaimini calculations, interpretations, boundaries,
and report contracts are green.

## Jaimini Positioning

Jaimini Predicta is the classical Jyotish specialist for soul role, visible
identity, destiny direction, relationship mirrors, career dharma, and sign-based
life chapters.

User-facing description:

> Jaimini Predicta reveals the deeper role your soul is playing in this life:
> your inner mission, visible identity, career direction, relationship karma,
> and the destiny chapters that activate these themes.

Room distinction:

- `Vedic Predicta`: what is promised in the chart through Parashari houses,
  grahas, dashas, vargas, panchang, yogas, and remedies.
- `KP Predicta`: what a specific event question shows through cusps, sub-lords,
  significators, promise/block, and timing readiness.
- `Jaimini Predicta`: what role the soul is playing, how the person is seen,
  what karakas guide them, and which Chara Dasha chapter is active.
- `Numerology Predicta`: what the name, birth code, and number cycles suggest.
- `Signature Predicta`: what confirmed visible signature traits reflect about
  self-expression, rhythm, and presentation.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

1. Do not rename Nadi files, screens, reports, or prompts to Jaimini without
   replacing the underlying method.
2. Do not leave user-facing `Nadi` copy anywhere after the migration is green.
3. Do not leave hidden Nadi prompt instructions in Predicta intelligence,
   report generation, QA gates, translation files, safety pages, or pricing copy.
4. Do not let Jaimini borrow KP cusp logic or Parashari-only claims as its main
   method.
5. Do not let Jaimini become a crowded technical classroom. The UI must be
   clean, calm, confident, welcoming, and prediction-led.
6. Do not ship Jaimini reports that explain what Jaimini is more than they
   explain the user.
7. Do not mark any phase green from code review alone.
8. Do not mark any report-affecting phase green without generated PDF artifacts.
9. Do not mark any UI phase green without desktop, tablet, mobile, and
   narrow-mobile runtime screenshots or equivalent visual evidence.
10. Do not let Life Atlas consume Jaimini until the Jaimini deterministic data
    contract and interpretation contract are audited.
11. Do not preserve Nadi report credits, Nadi product labels, or Nadi navigation
    labels under a Jaimini name without explicit migration mapping.
12. Every phase must be committed with a detailed commit message only after it is
    strictly audited green.

## Clean UI Contract

Every Jaimini screen must follow this order:

1. `Prediction first`: what this says about the user.
2. `Evidence second`: the karaka, Arudha, Karakamsha, Upapada, or Chara Dasha
   reason.
3. `Guidance third`: what to focus on now.
4. `Technical drawer last`: detailed tables only when the user opens them.

Jaimini screens must avoid:

- long walls of explanatory text
- method-schooling before interpretation
- crowded card grids
- too many CTAs in one area
- technical terms without immediate human meaning
- empty space caused by mismatched two-column layouts
- badges/chips touching boundaries
- report-like detail on app screens

Default app surfaces should show only the most useful layers:

- `Soul Planet`
- `Visible Identity`
- `Career Dharma`
- `Relationship Mirror`
- `Current Destiny Chapter`
- `Ask Jaimini Predicta`
- `Download Jaimini Report`

Power-user details must be progressive:

- Chara Karaka table
- Karakamsha / Swamsa chart
- Arudha Lagna details
- Upapada Lagna details
- Jaimini aspects
- Argala where implemented
- Chara Dasha timeline
- method notes and source boundaries

## Life Atlas Integration Promise

Life Atlas may use Jaimini as a core evidence layer after green audit.

Life Atlas must use Jaimini for:

- soul role
- destiny direction
- visible identity
- relationship mirror
- life-chapter timing through Chara Dasha
- career dharma where Amatyakaraka and Arudha evidence is clear

Life Atlas must not expose dense Jaimini tables in the main body. It should
translate Jaimini into life language:

- not: `Your Atmakaraka is Saturn at X degrees.`
- yes: `Your life repeatedly asks you to turn pressure into maturity, and to
  become trustworthy without becoming emotionally hardened.`
