## Unverified Anecdote Handling

The UK transfer story described by the user could not be independently verified.
Predicta still treats it as a product signal because it shows the kind of value
users share publicly:

- a concrete event
- a near timing window
- a real-world trigger
- a memorable outcome

Predicta must not claim `100%` certainty or copy testimonial-style guarantees.
Predicta must instead build a system that can say:

> A foreign work opportunity is more likely between March and June. The trigger
> may come through your existing company, a senior colleague, team restructuring,
> or a role opened by someone leaving. This looks more like work travel/transfer
> first, not permanent settlement immediately.

## AstroLokal Strengths Predicta Must Beat

- Human trust: verified astrologer profiles and live sessions.
- Simplicity: pay, ask, connect.
- Local language comfort.
- Event-specific topics.
- Low-friction first paid session.
- User memory around precise predictions.

## AstroLokal Weaknesses Predicta Must Exploit Ethically

- Per-minute pressure can make users rush.
- Wallet/recharge/session failures can destroy trust.
- Human astrologers can freestyle, contradict themselves, or push remedies.
- Session insight may disappear unless a transcript/summary is saved.
- Accuracy is anecdotal unless outcomes are tracked.

## Predicta Locked Response

Predicta must become harder to beat by combining:

1. Deterministic calculation evidence.
2. One primary Predicta intelligence layer.
3. Event-question refinement.
4. Cross-school agreement/conflict checks.
5. AI wording only after evidence exists.
6. Optional human astrologer review for paid precision readings.
7. Saved transcript and summary.
8. Prediction outcome tracking.
9. Clear credits/products without per-minute pressure.
