## Market Redline Summary

Predicta’s report weakness is not lack of calculations. The weakness is that
reports sound like schooling, scaffolding, or method documentation instead of
direct prediction and guidance.

The market proves three expectations:

1. Free reports must be substantial enough to earn trust.
2. Paid reports must feel personally authored, specific, and deeper.
3. Technical proof must support the reading, not replace it.

## Locked Report Formula

Every meaningful report section must follow:

```text
technical evidence
plain prediction
timing / current relevance
what helps
what blocks
what to do next
confidence / caution
```

Bad section:

```text
D10 represents career and professional status.
```

Acceptable section:

```text
Your career grows when responsibility is visible and measurable. The chart
supports steady authority-building more than shortcuts. The current timing
favors skill consolidation first, then public recognition after the pressure
period softens. Focus on disciplined output, not noisy expansion.
```

## Free Vs Paid Contract

Free reports must:

- Give direct, useful prediction.
- Include enough technical evidence to feel trustworthy.
- Stay concise but not thin.
- Avoid “upgrade to learn anything meaningful” behavior.
- End with one practical next-step/action plan.

Paid reports must:

- Give fuller diagnosis and timing.
- Explain contradictions and mixed evidence.
- Include deeper proof tables and appendices.
- Add practical guidance by life area.
- Feel like a premium written consultation, not a longer free report.

## Banned Report Behaviors

- Main sections that define the method instead of reading the person.
- Repeating “this report can and cannot claim” across pages.
- Repeating remedies in multiple sections.
- Generic phrases like “meaningful insight”, “substantial report”, or
  “chart-backed notes” without a concrete prediction.
- KP reports that primarily show D1/D9 Parashari charts.
- Numerology reports that include Kundli or graha evidence.
- Signature reports without confirmed signature traits.
- Life Atlas reports that expose school proof as the main emotional experience.
- Paid reports that only add length but not sharper prediction.
