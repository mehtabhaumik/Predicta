# Redline Ledger

## Non-Negotiable Product Redlines

- Do not implement reports before deterministic app and intelligence modules.
- Do not let AI calculate Dosh, Shrap, Yog, or Lal Kitab.
- Do not show a condition as present without chart evidence.
- Do not use `Dosha` or `Shrapa` as user-facing canonical wording.
- Do not say a user is cursed.
- Do not fear-sell remedies.
- Do not recommend expensive rituals as the default solution.
- Do not duplicate the same condition across Dosh, Shrap, Yog, and remedies as
  separate unrelated claims.
- Do not treat Lal Kitab as Parashari.
- Do not use Nadi Dosh outside compatibility/matching.
- Do not make health, death, legal, pregnancy, or medical certainty claims.
- Do not make Premium more frightening than Free.
- Do not hide uncertainty; use `weak`, `cancelled`, `pending evidence`, or `not
  present` honestly.
- Do not create a long app wall. Deep detail belongs in reports.

## Copy Redlines

Forbidden style:

- "You are cursed."
- "This guarantees failure."
- "You must buy this remedy."
- "This Dosh will ruin marriage/career/children."
- "Only Premium can save you."

Required style:

- "This is a karmic pressure indicator."
- "The evidence is weak/medium/strong."
- "This pattern can create friction in..."
- "This is reduced by..."
- "A simple dharma-based remedy is..."
- "Premium adds deeper timing, evidence, and a structured plan."

## Evidence Redlines

Every displayed item must eventually include:

- exact planets
- house/sign/nakshatra where available
- D1/D9/Chalit support where applicable
- dasha/transit activation where possible
- reduction or cancellation logic where applicable
- remedy source/type
- confidence

## Remedy Redlines

- Free remedies must be safe, simple, and dharma/karma based.
- Premium remedies must still be safe, practical, and non-exploitative.
- No harmful, illegal, medical, or financially coercive remedy is allowed.
- Lal Kitab remedies must be whitelisted before display.
- Consolidated remedy planning must prevent duplicate or conflicting remedies.
