# PREDICTA_MONETIZATION_PHASE_7_FAMILY_VAULT_ASSIGNMENT_AND_COMPARISON_LIMITS

## Green Criteria Audit

- Family Vault uses saved Kundlis as the source for family profiles.
- Web Family Vault can assign any saved Kundli to a family member role directly
  from the Family Vault page.
- Mobile Family Vault lets users label saved Kundlis and explicitly include or
  exclude them from the comparison.
- Family comparison eligibility is centralized:
  - `0-1` selected Kundlis are blocked.
  - `2-4` selected Kundlis are allowed.
  - `5+` selected Kundlis are blocked.
- Blocked states preserve the selected Kundlis and explain the next step.
- Predicta deterministic chat explains Family Vault comparison eligibility
  without AI credit.

## UX Rule

Pair Comparison remains exactly two charts. Family Karma Map is the wider
Family Vault comparison surface and accepts two to four saved Kundlis.
