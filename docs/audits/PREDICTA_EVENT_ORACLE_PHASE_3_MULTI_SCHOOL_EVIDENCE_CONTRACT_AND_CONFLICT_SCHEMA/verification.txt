PREDICTA_EVENT_ORACLE_PHASE_3_MULTI_SCHOOL_EVIDENCE_CONTRACT_AND_CONFLICT_SCHEMA

Strict audit checklist:

- Deterministic evidence contract exists and is exported.
- Evidence layers include Vedic, KP, Jaimini, Kundli Karma, Numerology, optional Signature, and optional Life Atlas.
- Each layer has a source label and method boundary.
- Agreement score and conflict score are computed from source evidence.
- Required missing evidence produces missing-evidence flags.
- Missing required evidence produces the explicit not_enough_evidence state.
- Conflicting evidence lowers confidence.
- AI is explicitly forbidden from filling missing evidence.
- Signature absence remains optional and does not block non-signature prediction contracts.

Verification commands are recorded in phase-3-manifest.json and rerun before the phase is committed.
