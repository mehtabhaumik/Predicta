# Predicta Competitor Response, Positioning, And Report Supremacy Strict Phases

This roadmap raises Predicta's standard after comparing Predicta against
AskSoma, YastroTalk, and Nebula. It is not a replacement for the existing report
roadmap. It is a stricter pre-report and post-report control layer that makes
sure Predicta beats the competitor bar before report phases are rerun.

The current report gates may be green, but those gates validate the current
contract. This roadmap updates the contract itself: Predicta must feel more
trustworthy than Nebula, calmer and more complete than AskSoma, and more
evidence-backed than YastroTalk without becoming a technical textbook.

  rebuild gates for Vedic, KP, Jaimini, Numerology, Signature, Life Atlas,

## Competitor Benchmark Lock

Predicta must beat these competitor strengths without copying their weaknesses.

| Competitor | Strongest Edge | Predicta Must Beat It By |
|---|---|---|
| AskSoma | Friendly AI Vedic mentor, emotional clarity, daily insight, simple positioning | Keeping Predicta calmer and more emotionally useful while offering stronger school separation, reports, family vault, Kundli Karma, and evidence-backed depth |
| YastroTalk | Heavy Vedic engine claims: KP, Jaimini, Ashtakavarga, divisional charts, dashas, AI chart reading | Showing visible calculation evidence without overwhelming users and proving Predicta is prediction-first, not generic |
| Nebula | Consumer mystic UX, relationship appeal, emotional self-discovery, compatibility | Staying more trustworthy, evidence-backed, and non-psychic while still feeling emotionally warm and personal |

Predicta's locked market position:

> Predicta is the premium evidence-backed astrology intelligence app for people
> who want real guidance, not fear, fluff, or per-minute astrologer pressure.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

1. Do not rerun report phases as a substitute for competitor response work.
2. Do not call this roadmap green by passing existing report gates alone.
3. Do not let Predicta sound like a toolkit, astrology course, QA document, or
   implementation contract.
4. Do not hide technical evidence. Preserve it, then immediately translate it
   into prediction, timing, support, caution, and action.
5. Do not use psychic-style claims, vague supernatural certainty, or
   performative mysticism. Predicta may feel mystical, but must remain
   evidence-backed.
6. Do not copy AskSoma's simplicity by weakening Predicta's specialist worlds.
   Predicta must stay clearly separated into Vedic, KP, Jaimini, Numerology,
   Signature, Life Atlas, and Kundli Karma.
7. Do not copy YastroTalk's engine-heavy pitch by drowning users in engine
   proof. Evidence must be visible but calm.
8. Do not copy Nebula's psychic/advisor confusion. Predicta is an astrology
   intelligence platform, not a psychic marketplace.
9. Every app surface must show the user what Predicta knows and what it can do
   next without making the user hunt.
10. Every report preview must tell the user what they will actually learn, not
    only what sections are included.
11. Free users must receive real value. Paid users receive deeper evidence,
    timing, contradiction handling, stronger synthesis, and richer reports.
12. Predicta chat must know the active world, active Kundli, report previews,
    generated report context, saved context, free/premium boundaries, and
    local-memory answers before using AI.
13. If local deterministic memory can answer, Predicta must avoid AI usage.
14. If AI is needed, Predicta must know why it is needed and preserve user
    context before upsell or credit gating.
15. Vedic, KP, Jaimini, Numerology, Signature, and Life Atlas reports must be
    rerun after this roadmap updates the product standard.
16. Every phase requires strict audit, generated evidence where affected, and a
    commit before the next phase.
17. If any phase finds a contradiction with an older roadmap, update the older
    roadmap or gate explicitly. Do not let roadmaps silently disagree.
18. No phase is green unless the final audit finds zero Critical and zero Major
    issues.

## Phase 0: `PREDICTA_COMPETITOR_RESPONSE_PHASE_0_BENCHMARK_REDLINE_AND_POSITION_LOCK`

### Goal

Lock the competitor-response standard before changing product copy, app
surfaces, Predicta intelligence, or reports.

### Must Audit

- AskSoma current public positioning, features, pricing/free value, AI mentor
  tone, trust copy, and emotional clarity.
- YastroTalk current public claims around AI, Vedic engines, KP, Jaimini,
  Ashtakavarga, divisional charts, dashas, and privacy.
- Nebula current public features around birth chart, compatibility, daily
  guidance, psychic/tarot/advisor surfaces, relationship positioning, and trust
  risks.
- Predicta current app copy, report page copy, report preview copy, chat
  behavior, specialist-room copy, and generated report artifacts.

### Must Produce

- `docs/audits/PREDICTA_COMPETITOR_RESPONSE_PHASE_0_BENCHMARK_REDLINE_AND_POSITION_LOCK/competitor-benchmark.md`
- Source matrix with URLs, observed competitor strength, Predicta risk,
  Predicta response, and what Predicta rejects.
- Redline ledger for:
  - generic report language
  - toolkit/schooling language
  - engine-heavy overwhelm
  - psychic confusion
  - weak free value
  - paid depth that only adds pages
  - missing Predicta memory/context
- Updated positioning statement for app, report pages, support copy, and
  Predicta memory.

### Green Criteria

- Source-backed benchmark exists.
- Predicta's locked positioning is present in the audit artifact.
- The audit names exact app/report/intelligence areas to update.
- No implementation is done in this phase except audit artifacts and roadmap
  alignment.

## Phase 1: `PREDICTA_COMPETITOR_RESPONSE_PHASE_1_BRAND_PROMISE_COPY_AND_NAVIGATION_ALIGNMENT`

### Goal

Make every high-level product surface communicate Predicta's promise clearly:
evidence-backed astrology intelligence, direct guidance, no fear, no fluff, no
per-minute pressure.

### Must Update

- Landing page hero and value proposition.
- Login/signup trust copy.
- Dashboard welcome copy.
- Specialist-world entry cards.
- Report marketplace intro.
- Pricing/pass descriptions where they explain AI/report value.
- Account/support trust copy where relevant.
- Translation JSON files for all user-facing copy touched.

### Must Not Do

- Do not hardcode translations in components.
- Do not introduce Nadi as an active final lane.
- Do not use vague phrases like `unlock your destiny` without grounding them in
  evidence-backed guidance.
- Do not make the app sound defensive or over-explanatory.

### Green Criteria

- Brand promise appears consistently without repetitive copy.
- User can understand within five seconds what Predicta does better than
  competitors.
- No overflow, clipping, CTA collision, or mobile density regression on touched
  routes.
- Translation/no-hardcoded-copy sweep passes for touched copy.

## Phase 2: `PREDICTA_COMPETITOR_RESPONSE_PHASE_2_SPECIALIST_WORLD_CLARITY_AND_NO_MIXING_GATE`

### Goal

Ensure all worlds are clearly separated and user-friendly:

- Vedic: classical Kundli, charts, dasha, gochar, Panchang, Kundli Karma,
  remedies.
- KP: event answer and timing through cusp/sub-lord/significator evidence.
- Jaimini: soul direction, karakas, Karakamsha, Arudha, Chara Dasha, destiny
  patterns.
- Numerology: number identity, name rhythm, cycles, compatibility where
  appropriate.
- Signature: reflective visible-trait self-expression, not forensic prediction.
- Life Atlas: approved synthesis lane only.

### Must Audit

- Web specialist rooms.
- Mobile specialist rooms.
- Report marketplace lanes.
- Chat routing and handoff prompts.
- Report generation payloads.
- Predicta memory/context taxonomy.

### Green Criteria

- No school uses another school's chart/report shell.
- KP does not show D1/D9 Parashari as the primary KP report chart.
- Jaimini does not mention Nadi as active product taxonomy.
- Numerology does not lead with Kundli/Dasha identity.
- Signature cannot report without confirmed traits.
- Life Atlas is the only synthesis path.

## Phase 4: `PREDICTA_COMPETITOR_RESPONSE_PHASE_4_APP_SURFACE_PREDICTION_FIRST_UX_REBUILD`

### Goal

Make app screens feel more useful than competitors without becoming crowded.
Every specialist surface must lead with what the user should know, not a lesson.

### Must Update

- Vedic overview and Kundli Karma cards.
- KP question flow and answer preview.
- Jaimini overview.
- Numerology dashboard.
- Signature scan/trait confirmation.
- Life Atlas preview.
- Daily/current timing surfaces where relevant.

### UX Rules

- Start with prediction/guidance.
- Show evidence underneath, collapsible where dense.
- Keep top active signals visible.
- Use progressive disclosure.
- Keep CTA near the user's action.
- Avoid long reading walls; PDF remains the deep reading surface.

### Green Criteria

- Desktop, tablet, mobile, and narrow-mobile screenshots are captured where
  touched.
- No text overflow, clipping, cramped chips, CTA collisions, or horizontal
  scroll.
- Each touched page has a clear primary action.
- Free users see useful value without a wall of text.
